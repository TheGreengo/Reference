# Pancakes (Without Buttermilk)

## Ingredients
 - 2 cups flour
 - 1/4 cup sugar
 - 4 tsp baking powder
 - 1/4 tsp baking soda
 - 1/2 tsp salt
 - 1 3/4 cups milk
 - 1/4 cup butter
 - 2 tsp vanilla
 - 1 large egg

## Instructions
 1. Sift together the dry ingredients
 2. Mix together the wet ingredients
 3. Fold the wets into the dries
